# Ransomware Investigation

## Objective

Identify, contain, and remediate ransomware activity.

## Investigation Steps

1. Identify impacted devices.
2. Review Defender detections.
3. Determine ransomware family.
4. Review encryption activity.
5. Investigate lateral movement.
6. Identify initial access vector.
7. Assess business impact.

## Containment

- Isolate affected systems.
- Disable impacted accounts.
- Block malicious indicators.

## Remediation

- Remove malicious artifacts.
- Restore systems from backup.
- Patch vulnerabilities.

## Closure Criteria

- Threat eradicated.
- Recovery completed.
- No persistence detected.
