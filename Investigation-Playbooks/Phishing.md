# Phishing Investigation

## Objective

Determine whether a phishing email poses a risk to users or the organization.

## Investigation Steps

1. Review sender information.
2. Analyze email headers.
3. Review URLs and attachments.
4. Check user interaction.
5. Determine campaign scope.
6. Search for additional recipients.
7. Assess business impact.

## Potential Indicators of Compromise

- Malicious links.
- Credential harvesting pages.
- Malware attachments.
- Suspicious domains.

## Containment

- Remove malicious messages.
- Block sender.
- Block domains and URLs.
- Notify affected users.

## Remediation

- Reset affected credentials.
- Review user activity.
- Deploy awareness communication.

## Closure Criteria

- Threat contained.
- Malicious content blocked.
- Impact assessed and documented.
