# Suspected Identity Theft Investigation

## Objective

Determine whether a user account has been compromised and is being used by an unauthorized individual.

## Investigation Steps

1. Review sign-in logs.
2. Identify unusual locations.
3. Check MFA status.
4. Review device information.
5. Investigate risky sign-ins.
6. Look for impossible travel events.
7. Validate activity with account owner.

## Potential Indicators of Compromise

- Unexpected successful sign-ins.
- New devices.
- Multiple geographic locations.
- Unusual user behavior.

## Containment

- Disable account.
- Revoke active sessions.
- Force password reset.
- Require MFA re-registration.

## Remediation

- Review affected resources.
- Monitor account activity.
- Investigate related accounts.

## Closure Criteria

- User identity verified.
- Account secured.
- No ongoing malicious activity detected.
