# Privileged Access Investigation

## Objective

Validate the legitimacy of privileged access assignments and identify potential misuse.

## Investigation Steps

1. Identify assigned role or group.
2. Review approval records.
3. Verify business justification.
4. Review assignment duration.
5. Check related user activities.
6. Review audit logs.
7. Validate request with owner if required.

## Potential Indicators of Compromise

- Unauthorized access assignment.
- Unapproved role changes.
- Access outside business requirements.
- Excessive privilege usage.

## Containment

- Remove unauthorized access.
- Disable affected accounts if required.
- Review privileged activities.

## Remediation

- Validate approval processes.
- Perform access review.
- Implement additional monitoring.

## Closure Criteria

- Assignment approved.
- Business justification confirmed.
- No suspicious activity detected.
